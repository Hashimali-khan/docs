## Get Started in 4 Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create your free UpForge account using your email or social login. No credit card needed — you're ready to go in under a minute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Course">
    Browse the catalog and pick a project-based course that matches your goals. Each course is packed with hands-on video lessons you can follow at your own pace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch Lessons">
    Work through video lessons one by one. Mark each lesson complete as you go and watch your progress bar fill up in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn XP & Achievements">
    Every completed lesson awards XP. Level up, build daily streaks, unlock achievements, and see your name rise on the leaderboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

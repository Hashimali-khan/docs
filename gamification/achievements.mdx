Achievements are milestone rewards that unlock automatically when you hit certain goals on UpForge. Each achievement awards a one-time bonus XP payout and a badge you can display on your profile. There's no manual claiming required — just keep learning and UpForge takes care of the rest.

## Available Achievements

UpForge currently has three built-in achievements:

| Achievement | Description | Criteria | Bonus XP |
|---|---|---|---|
| 📚 Lesson Explorer | Complete 5 lessons | Complete 5 lessons across any courses | 100 XP |
| 🎓 Course Master | Complete your first course | Finish every lesson in any single course | 200 XP |
| 🔥 Streak Starter | Maintain a 7-day streak | Complete at least one lesson on 7 consecutive days | 150 XP |

## How Achievements are Unlocked

UpForge checks your progress automatically after **every lesson completion**. You don't need to visit the Achievements page or take any manual action — if you've met the criteria for an achievement, it's awarded immediately.

Here's what happens when you complete a lesson:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Lesson is marked complete">
    Your progress is saved and your XP is updated with the lesson's point value.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your stats are evaluated">
    UpForge counts your total lessons completed, courses completed, and current streak, then checks each achievement's criteria against those numbers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Eligible achievements are awarded">
    Any achievement you've newly qualified for is unlocked and its bonus XP is added to your total automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Each achievement can only be earned once. Already-earned achievements are skipped during future checks.

## Viewing Your Achievements

Navigate to the **Achievements** page from the sidebar to see a full overview of your progress.

The page is split into three tabs:

| Tab | What you see |
|---|---|
| **All Achievements** | Every achievement, earned and locked, in a single grid |
| **Earned** | Only the achievements you've already unlocked |
| **Locked** | Achievements still waiting to be unlocked |

Earned achievements appear in full colour with the date you unlocked them. Locked achievements are shown in a dimmed state so you can see exactly what you're working toward.

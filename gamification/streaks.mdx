A streak tracks how many consecutive days you've been actively learning on UpForge. Every day you complete at least one lesson, your streak grows. It's a simple but powerful way to build a consistent learning habit and stay motivated to show up every day.

## How a Streak is Counted

UpForge counts your streak by looking at unique calendar days on which you completed at least one lesson. Here's how it works step by step:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete a lesson">
    Mark any lesson as complete. The date of that completion is recorded automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="UpForge counts your active days">
    Your streak is the number of consecutive calendar days — counting back from today or yesterday — that each have at least one completed lesson.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your streak updates immediately">
    After every lesson completion, UpForge recalculates your current streak and longest streak in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep it going tomorrow">
    Return the next day and complete at least one more lesson to extend your streak by one more day.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When your streak is active, it appears on your dashboard and leaderboard entry as a flame indicator — for example:

> 🔥 **7 Day Streak!**

UpForge tracks two streak values for you:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Current Streak** | Your unbroken run of active days right now |
| **Longest Streak** | Your all-time personal best consecutive-day record |

## Streak Rules

Keep these rules in mind to avoid unexpectedly losing your streak:

- **One lesson is enough.** Completing a single lesson in a day is all it takes to keep your streak alive. You don't need to hit your full daily goal.
- **Multiple lessons in one day still count as one streak day.** Binge-watching three lessons on Saturday adds one day to your streak, not three.
- **Days are based on UTC.** A calendar day runs from midnight to midnight UTC. If you're in a different time zone, plan accordingly so you don't miss a day at the boundary.
- **Your streak is recalculated automatically** after every lesson completion — you never need to manually update anything.

<Warning>
  Missing a single day **resets your streak to zero**. There is no grace period or streak freeze. Make sure you complete at least one lesson every day to protect your current streak.
</Warning>

## Streak Achievement

Maintaining a consistent streak unlocks a dedicated achievement:

<Card title="🔥 Streak Starter — 150 XP" icon="fire">
  Maintain a streak of **7 consecutive days** to earn the Streak Starter achievement and a one-time bonus of **150 XP**. This is awarded automatically the moment your streak hits day 7.
</Card>

---

## Streak on the Leaderboard

Your current streak is displayed right next to your name, level, and XP on the [Leaderboard](/gamification/leaderboard). Even if your XP rank isn't at the top yet, a long streak signals consistency and dedication to everyone on the platform.

## Tracking Today's Progress

<Tip>
  The **Today's Goal** card on your dashboard shows how many lessons you've completed today versus the 3-lesson daily target. Use it as a quick check to make sure you've done at least one lesson before the day ends and your streak is safe.
</Tip>

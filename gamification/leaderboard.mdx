The leaderboard ranks all UpForge learners by total XP earned. It updates in real time as learners complete lessons and unlock achievements, so every session counts. Head to the **Leaderboard** page from the sidebar to see where you stand.

## Your Rank Card

At the very top of the Leaderboard page, a highlighted card shows your personal stats at a glance — no need to scroll through the full list to find yourself. Your rank card displays:

- **Your current rank** — your position among all ranked learners (e.g. `#12`)
- **Your total XP** — your current points total
- **Your level** — derived from your XP using the [level formula](/gamification/xp-and-levels#level-formula)
- **Your active streak** — current consecutive-day streak displayed as a badge (e.g. `🔥 5 day streak`)

Your rank card appears in a distinct purple gradient so it's always easy to spot.

---

## Top 3 Podium

The top three learners get a special podium treatment above the main rankings list. Each podium card shows the learner's avatar, display name, XP total, level, and current streak. The medals are awarded as follows:

| Place | Medal |
|---|---|
| 1st | 🥇 Gold — highlighted with a gold border |
| 2nd | 🥈 Silver |
| 3rd | 🥉 Bronze |

Aim for a podium spot by completing lessons and stacking achievement bonuses for the biggest XP gains.

---

## Full Rankings

Below the podium, all remaining ranked learners are listed in order. Each row in the full rankings shows:

- **Rank number** — your numerical position in the global standings
- **Avatar** — your profile picture or auto-generated initial
- **Display name** — your name or username
- **Level** — your current level
- **Streak** — your current consecutive-day learning streak (🔥)
- **XP** — your total points, displayed in purple

  <Card title="Ranked by total XP" icon="trophy" href="/gamification/xp-and-levels">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Up to 100 users displayed" icon="list-ol" href="/gamification/leaderboard">

Your rank updates automatically each time you (or anyone else) completes a lesson or earns an achievement — there is no manual refresh needed.

## Improving Your Rank

The fastest ways to climb the leaderboard:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete more lessons">
    Each lesson awards XP equal to the course's total points divided by its number of lessons. Work through courses consistently to keep your XP climbing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish full courses">
    Completing an entire course unlocks the 🎓 **Course Master** achievement for a **200 XP** bonus on top of per-lesson awards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock achievements">
    Each of the three achievements awards a one-time XP bonus (100–200 XP). Stack them to jump multiple ranks at once. See [Achievements](/gamification/achievements) for the full list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Maintaining a long streak won't directly increase your XP rank — only total points determine your position. However, your streak is prominently displayed next to your name on the leaderboard, making it a great way to show consistency and stand out even while you're climbing the XP ranks.
</Tip>

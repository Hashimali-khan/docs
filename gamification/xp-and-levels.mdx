XP (experience points) drive your progression on UpForge. Every lesson you complete and every achievement you unlock adds to your total XP, raising your level and pushing you up the leaderboard. The more XP you earn, the higher your level — and the more your dedication shows.

## Earning XP

There are two ways to earn XP on UpForge:

### 1. Completing Lessons

Each course on UpForge has a total point value. When you complete a lesson, UpForge awards you a share of that course's points, calculated by dividing the course's total XP evenly across all its lessons:

> **XP per lesson** = floor(course total XP ÷ number of lessons in the course)

For example, a course worth 500 XP with 10 lessons awards you **50 XP** per lesson completed. Points are credited to your account automatically the moment you mark a lesson as complete.

### 2. Unlocking Achievements

Hitting milestone achievements grants you a one-time bonus XP award on top of your per-lesson earnings. Current achievement bonuses include:

| Achievement | Bonus XP |
|---|---|
| 📚 Lesson Explorer | 100 XP |
| 🔥 Streak Starter | 150 XP |
| 🎓 Course Master | 200 XP |

See [Achievements](/gamification/achievements) for the full list of criteria and how to unlock each one.

## Level Formula

Your level is calculated directly from your total XP. The two formulas that drive your progression are:

> **Current level** = floor(√(total XP ÷ 100)) + 1

> **XP needed to reach next level** = (current level)² × 100

Each level requires progressively more XP than the last — the gap between levels widens as you advance. Your level increases automatically as your XP crosses each threshold; there is nothing to activate or claim.

Here's what the thresholds look like in practice:

| Level | XP Required |
|---|---|
| 1 | 0 XP |
| 2 | 100 XP |
| 3 | 400 XP |
| 4 | 900 XP |
| 5 | 1,600 XP |
| 10 | 8,100 XP |

---

## Viewing Your Level

Your current level appears in two places:

- **Dashboard** — The **Total XP** card displays your total XP alongside your current level badge. Check it any time to see how close you are to your next level.
- **Leaderboard** — Every entry on the [Leaderboard](/gamification/leaderboard) shows each learner's level next to their XP total, so you can compare your progress at a glance.

<Tip>
  Complete achievement milestones for large one-time XP boosts. Stacking lesson XP with achievement bonuses is the fastest way to climb levels — especially early on when thresholds are low.
</Tip>

Your dashboard is your learning home base. Every time you open UpForge, this is where you land — a single, at-a-glance view of your progress, your active courses, your recent activity, and the gamification stats that keep you motivated. Everything you need to decide what to do next is right here.

<Tip>
  UpForge defaults to **dark mode** for a comfortable, low-eye-strain experience. Toggle between dark and light themes at any time using the theme button in the top-right header bar.
</Tip>

## Stats bar

At the top of the main content area, four animated **bento stat cards** summarise your learning at a glance. Each card updates in real time as you complete lessons.

| Card | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Total XP / Level** | Your cumulative experience points and your current level (e.g. "Level 3"). XP is earned by completing lessons and unlocking achievements. |
| **Active Courses** | The number of courses you are currently enrolled in but haven't yet completed. Finished courses appear in a separate "completed" count beneath the active number. |
| **Current Streak** | How many consecutive days you have completed at least one lesson. Miss a day and the streak resets to 0, so keep the flame alive! |
| **Today's Goal** | A progress bar tracking your daily lesson target of **3 lessons**. The bar fills from 0 to 100 % as you tick off lessons throughout the day. |

## Active courses grid

Below the stats bar, the **Jump Back In** section displays all the courses you are actively enrolled in. Each course card shows:

- The course **thumbnail** (with a play-button overlay on hover)
- The course **title**, **duration** (in minutes), and **difficulty level**
- A **progress bar** so you can see how far through the course you are at a glance

Click any card to jump straight into the course and pick up where you left off. If you have no active enrolments yet, this section is hidden — head to the Courses catalogue to enrol in your first course.

## Recent activity panel

The right-hand column of the dashboard contains the **Recent Activity** panel. It lists the last five lessons you completed across all your courses, showing:

- The **lesson title** and the **course it belongs to**
- The **date** the lesson was completed

This feed updates immediately after you finish a lesson. If you haven't completed any lessons yet, the panel shows a friendly prompt to complete your first lesson and get started.

## Navigation sidebar

The collapsible sidebar on the left gives you quick access to every major section of UpForge. On desktop it is always visible; on mobile it becomes a bottom navigation bar.

| Link | Destination |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | `/dashboard` — this page |
| **Courses** | `/courses` — browse and enrol in new courses |
| **Leaderboard** | `/leaderboard` — see how you rank against other learners |
| **Achievements** | `/achievements` — view all earned and locked achievements |

A **Sign Out** button sits at the bottom of the sidebar for when you need to log out.

The User API provides two endpoints that power your personal dashboard. `GET /api/user/stats` gives you a comprehensive snapshot of your learning progress, gamification stats, and recent activity. `POST /api/user/sync` keeps your UpForge profile in sync with your authentication account — updating your name, email, and avatar whenever they change.

---

## GET /api/user/stats

Returns your complete dashboard stats in a single call — XP, level, streaks, course counts, today's progress toward your daily goal, and a feed of your five most recent lesson completions.

### Response fields

<ResponseField name="username" type="string" required>
  Your display name. Resolved in priority order: username → first name → email prefix. Falls back to `"Learner"` if none is available.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="level" type="number" required>
  Your current level, calculated from your total XP using UpForge's levelling formula.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="totalXP" type="number" required>
  Your total accumulated XP, including lesson XP and all achievement bonuses.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="nextLevelPoints" type="number" required>
  The XP threshold you need to reach in order to advance to the next level.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="currentStreak" type="number" required>
  Number of consecutive calendar days on which you've completed at least one lesson. Resets to `0` if you miss a day.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="longestStreak" type="number" required>
  The highest `currentStreak` value you have ever reached.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="totalLessonsCompleted" type="number" required>
  Cumulative count of lessons you have marked complete across all courses.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="coursesInProgress" type="number" required>
  Number of courses you are enrolled in but have not yet fully completed.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="completedCourses" type="number" required>
  Number of courses whose enrollment record is marked as completed.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="todayProgress" type="number" required>
  Integer percentage (0–100) representing your progress toward today's daily goal of **3 lessons**. Capped at `100` even if you exceed the goal.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="todayCompleted" type="number" required>
  Number of lessons you have completed today (since midnight).
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="remainingToday" type="number" required>
  Lessons still needed to hit the 3-lesson daily goal. `0` once the goal is reached.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="recentActivity" type="RecentActivity[]" required>
  Your last 5 completed lessons, ordered newest first.

  <Expandable title="RecentActivity object fields">
    <ResponseField name="id" type="string">UUID of the progress record.</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="title" type="string">Title of the completed lesson.</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="courseTitle" type="string">Title of the course the lesson belongs to.</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="completedAt" type="string | null">ISO 8601 timestamp of when you completed the lesson.</ResponseField>
  </Expandable>
</ResponseField>

### Error responses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `401`  | You are not authenticated. |

<Note>
  If your UpForge user record doesn't exist yet, the endpoint returns a valid zero-state response rather than an error. Call `POST /api/user/sync` first to ensure your record is created.
</Note>

  "level": 5,
  "totalXP": 1850,
  "nextLevelPoints": 2500,
  "currentStreak": 3,

      "id": "prog-uuid-001",

## POST /api/user/sync

Syncs your authentication profile (name, email, username, and avatar URL) into UpForge. If no record exists for your account yet, one is created. If a record already exists, it is updated with any changed values from your auth profile.

No request body is needed — all data is read from your active session.

### Response fields

<ResponseField name="success" type="boolean" required>
  `true` when the sync completed (whether your record was created or updated).
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="user" type="object" required>
  The full user record as it exists after the sync.

  <Expandable title="User object fields">
    <ResponseField name="id" type="string">Your internal UpForge UUID.</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="clerkId" type="string">Your authentication provider user ID.</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="email" type="string">Your email address.</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="name" type="string | null">Your display name. Derived from first + last name, username, or email prefix.</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="username" type="string | null">Your username, or email prefix if no username is set.</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="avatarUrl" type="string | null">URL of your profile picture.</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="points" type="number">Your total XP (unchanged by a sync).</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="level" type="number">Your current level (unchanged by a sync).</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="currentStreak" type="number">Your current streak (unchanged by a sync).</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="longestStreak" type="number">Your longest streak (unchanged by a sync).</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="lastActive" type="string | null">Timestamp of your last recorded lesson activity.</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="createdAt" type="string">ISO 8601 timestamp of when your account was first created in UpForge.</ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="updatedAt" type="string">ISO 8601 timestamp of the most recent update to your record.</ResponseField>
  </Expandable>
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="message" type="string" required>
  `"User created successfully"` for new accounts, `"User updated successfully"` for existing accounts.
</ResponseField>

### Error responses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `401`  | You are not authenticated or your session could not be read. |
| `500`  | An unexpected error occurred. The response body will be `{ "error": "Failed to sync user" }`. |

<Tip>
  The UpForge dashboard calls `POST /api/user/sync` automatically on every page load. You only need to call it yourself if you're building an integration that creates or updates user sessions outside the normal dashboard flow.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  A sync **only** updates profile information (name, email, avatar, username). It never resets gamification data such as `points`, `level`, or streak values — those are managed exclusively through the [Progress API](/api-reference/endpoints/progress).
</Warning>

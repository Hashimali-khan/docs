UpForge gives you a clear, real-time picture of your learning at every level — from individual lessons all the way up to your total XP and level. Your progress data updates the moment you mark a lesson complete, so your dashboard, course cards, and stats always reflect your current standing.

## Per-Course Progress

Inside the course player, the **curriculum sidebar** displays a progress bar at the top showing what percentage of the course you have completed. The percentage is calculated as:

```
progress % = (completed lessons ÷ total lessons) × 100
```

The bar fills with a smooth animation each time you complete a lesson, giving you instant visual feedback.

On the **course catalog** and your **dashboard**, enrolled courses show the same progress percentage directly on their course card, so you can see where you stand across all active courses at a glance.

## Daily Goal

Your **Today's Goal** card on the dashboard tracks how many lessons you have completed today against a daily target of **3 lessons**.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Lessons completed today" icon="calendar-check">
    A counter shows `X / 3 lessons` — the exact number you have finished today alongside the daily target.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Goal progress bar" icon="chart-bar">
    A progress bar fills in real time as you complete lessons. Completing all 3 lessons brings the bar to 100 %.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The daily goal resets at midnight each day. The remaining lessons needed to hit the goal (`remainingToday`) also appears in your stats so you always know how close you are.

## Recent Activity Feed

The right column of your dashboard displays a **Recent Activity** feed showing the last 5 lessons you completed across all courses. Each entry includes:

- The **lesson title**
- The **course it belongs to**
- The **timestamp** of when you completed it

This feed is a quick way to review what you have been working on and to spot any gaps in your learning cadence.

## Stats Overview

The top of your dashboard shows four summary stats cards that aggregate your activity across the entire platform:

| Stat | What it tracks |
|---|---|
| **Total XP** | Your cumulative experience points from all completed lessons and unlocked achievements |
| **Level** | Your current level, calculated from your total XP |
| **Active Courses** | The number of courses you are currently enrolled in but have not yet finished |
| **Completed Courses** | The number of courses where you have finished every lesson |

Your level and XP are also displayed with a progress bar showing how many XP you need to reach the next level.

<Tip>
  Completing 3 lessons every day is the fastest way to keep your daily streak alive. Each day you hit your 3-lesson goal counts as an active day, which advances your streak counter. Missing a day resets the streak, so building a consistent daily habit — even just 3 short lessons — is more valuable than binge-watching one day and skipping the next.
</Tip>

Enrolling in a course on UpForge takes a single click and has no prerequisites, cost, or approval step. Once you are enrolled, every lesson in that course is unlocked, your progress is tracked, and the course appears on your dashboard so you can jump back in any time. You can enroll in as many courses as you like simultaneously.

## How to Enroll

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a course from the catalog">
    Navigate to **Courses** in the sidebar to open the catalog. Click any course card to open its detail and player page.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Click the "Enroll Now" button'>
    On the course page, find the **Enroll Now** button in the course header banner. Click it. UpForge creates your enrollment record immediately — no confirmation screen or waiting period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start watching — all lessons are now accessible">
    As soon as enrollment is confirmed, every lesson in the curriculum sidebar unlocks. Select any lesson to begin watching. Your progress is tracked from this point forward.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens When You Enroll

When you click **Enroll Now**, UpForge performs the following actions behind the scenes:

- **Creates an enrollment record** linking your account to the course.
- **Unlocks all lessons** in the curriculum sidebar (previously only the first lesson was accessible to non-enrolled visitors).
- **Adds the course to your Active Courses** section on the dashboard so you can resume it from the "Jump Back In" grid without navigating back through the catalog.
- **Enables progress tracking** — every lesson you mark complete is recorded and reflected in the course progress bar.

## Unenrolling from a Course

If you change your mind, you can unenroll at any time:

1. Open the course player page for the course you want to leave.
2. Click the **Enrolled** button in the course header banner. It changes to a loading state briefly, then confirms the removal.
3. The course is removed from your Active Courses on the dashboard. Any lesson completion records you already earned are retained.

There is no penalty for unenrolling. You can re-enroll in the same course later and pick up where you left off.

## Enrolled vs. Not Enrolled Experience

| Feature | Not enrolled | Enrolled |
|---|---|---|
| View course description | ✅ | ✅ |
| Watch first lesson (preview) | ✅ | ✅ |
| Watch all lessons | ❌ | ✅ |
| Mark lessons complete | ❌ | ✅ |
| Earn XP for lessons | ❌ | ✅ |
| See progress bar in sidebar | ❌ | ✅ |
| Course appears on dashboard | ❌ | ✅ |

<Note>
  **Auto-enrollment:** You do not have to click Enroll before you start learning. If you mark a lesson complete directly in the course player and you are not yet enrolled, UpForge automatically enrolls you in that course before recording the completion. This means you never lose credit for a lesson you complete.
</Note>

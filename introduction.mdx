**UpForge** is a gamified online learning platform built for developers, creators, and curious minds who want to build real skills — and actually stay motivated doing it. Instead of passive video watching, you earn XP for every lesson you complete, level up as you grow, maintain daily streaks to build consistent habits, and unlock achievements that mark your progress. Whether you're picking up a new programming language or deepening your craft, UpForge turns the hard work of learning into something you genuinely look forward to.

<Note>
  UpForge is free to join. No credit card required.
</Note>

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Project-Based Courses" icon="book-open" href="/learning/browse-courses">
    Every course is built around real-world projects. You don't just watch — you build, apply, and ship, so skills stick long after the video ends.
  </Card>
  <Card title="XP & Levels" icon="star" href="/gamification/xp-and-levels">
    Earn experience points each time you complete a lesson. Your XP total determines your level, which rises as you learn more. Level up with the formula: `level = floor(sqrt(XP / 100)) + 1`.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Daily Streaks" icon="fire" href="/gamification/streaks">
    Complete at least one lesson every day to keep your streak alive. The longer your streak, the more impressive your profile — and the harder you'll work to protect it.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Achievements" icon="medal" href="/gamification/achievements">
    Hit key milestones — like completing your first course or maintaining a 7-day streak — to unlock achievement badges and earn bonus XP.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Leaderboard" icon="trophy" href="/gamification/leaderboard">
    See how you stack up against every learner on the platform. The global leaderboard ranks users by total XP, so the more you learn, the higher you climb.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dashboard" icon="gauge" href="/getting-started/dashboard-overview">
    Your personal command center. Track today's lesson goal, view your current streak, monitor XP progress toward the next level, and review recent activity — all in one place.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your free account">
    Sign up at UpForge using your email or a social login provider. Your account is created instantly — no credit card, no friction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the course catalog and enroll">
    Head to the **Courses** page to explore available project-based courses. Read the course description and hit **Enroll** to add it to your learning list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete video lessons and earn XP">
    Open any course and work through its video lessons at your own pace. Mark each lesson complete when you finish it — UpForge automatically awards XP, updates your streak, and checks whether you've unlocked any new achievements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your progress, unlock achievements, and climb the leaderboard">
    Return to your **Dashboard** anytime to see today's progress, your current streak, and XP toward the next level. Visit **Achievements** to view every badge you've earned and what's still locked. Check the **Leaderboard** to see your global ranking among all learners.
  </Step>
</Steps>
